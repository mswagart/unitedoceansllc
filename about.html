About United Oceans

Decades of International Maritime and Commercial Experience

A global perspective shaped by overseas office development, professional assignments across major commercial centers, and long-term relationships built across every principal trading region.

Global Experience. Strategic Relationships. Practical Commercial Solutions.

United Oceans LLC draws upon decades of international experience in maritime commerce, international trade, commodity sourcing, commodity trading and global logistics. Built on decades of hands-on leadership in international shipping and global commerce, United Oceans delivers practical commercial solutions to clients operating in today's global marketplace.

The experience behind the company includes establishing shipping operations throughout the United States, South America, Europe, the Middle East and the Far East; helping establish and expand major international shipping organizations; coordinating personnel, cargoes and transportation assets to create efficient global logistics networks; and fostering long-standing strategic relationships across international markets.

Over the years, this experience has encompassed the international movement of millions of tons of cargo, the purchase and sale of new and second-hand vessels, the commercial management and demolition of several hundred vessels, and the development of international sourcing, procurement and commodity trading programs for industrial products, food ingredients and agricultural commodities.

This breadth of experience enables United Oceans to bridge international markets, connect reliable business partners, and transform complex commercial opportunities into practical, executable solutions.

Today, United Oceans combines global experience, strategic relationships and practical industry expertise to help clients source products, strengthen supply chains, optimize logistics, structure international transactions and identify new business opportunities. Whether supporting a single transaction or developing a long-term commercial relationship, United Oceans is committed to delivering practical, commercially sound solutions that create lasting value for clients worldwide.

Experience. Integrity. Commercial Judgment. Trusted Execution.
